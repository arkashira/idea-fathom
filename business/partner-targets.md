# partner-targets.md

## Partner Integration Roadmap for Idea-Fathom

### Target Integrations

1. **Zapier**
   - **Rationale**: Zapier allows users to automate workflows by connecting different apps. Indie hackers can automate their ideation process by linking Idea-Fathom with other tools they use.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: 5 Zaps, 100 tasks/month
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium
   - **Value-Add**: Streamlines the ideation process by automating data flow between Idea-Fathom and other SaaS tools (e.g., Trello, Google Sheets).

2. **Airtable**
   - **Rationale**: Airtable is a powerful database tool that can help indie hackers organize and manage their ideas and market research.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: 1,200 records per base, 2 GB attachment space
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium
   - **Value-Add**: Provides a structured way to store and analyze generated ideas, enhancing the validation process.

3. **Notion**
   - **Rationale**: Notion is a versatile workspace that can be used for documentation, project management, and idea tracking.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: Unlimited pages and blocks for individuals
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium
   - **Value-Add**: Allows users to document and manage their ideas in a collaborative environment, facilitating team discussions and feedback.

4. **Stripe**
   - **Rationale**: Integrating with Stripe can help indie hackers monetize their ideas directly through Idea-Fathom.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: No monthly fees; transaction fees apply
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium
   - **Value-Add**: Enables users to validate their ideas by setting up payment systems for pre-orders or subscriptions.

5. **Figma**
   - **Rationale**: Figma is a collaborative design tool that can help indie hackers visualize their ideas.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: 3 projects, unlimited files in drafts
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium
   - **Value-Add**: Facilitates the transition from ideation to prototyping, allowing users to create mockups of their software ideas.

6. **Google Analytics**
   - **Rationale**: Google Analytics can provide insights into user behavior and market trends, which can inform the ideation process.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: Up to 10 million hits/month
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium
   - **Value-Add**: Helps users validate their ideas based on real user data and trends, enhancing decision-making.

7. **Slack**
   - **Rationale**: Slack can be used for team communication and collaboration, allowing indie hackers to discuss and refine their ideas.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: 10,000 messages, 1:1 video calls
   - **Integration Effort**: Small
   - **Value-Add**: Facilitates real-time discussions and feedback on generated ideas, enhancing collaboration.

8. **Product Hunt**
   - **Rationale**: Integrating with Product Hunt can help indie hackers launch their ideas and gain visibility in the creator economy.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: N/A (community-driven platform)
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium
   - **Value-Add**: Provides a platform for validation and feedback from a community of early adopters, enhancing the exposure of new ideas.

### Summary
The integrations listed above are prioritized based on their potential to enhance the user experience of Idea-Fathom, focusing on automation, organization, monetization, visualization, and community engagement. Each integration has been evaluated for its free-tier limits, integration effort, and the specific user jobs it addresses, ensuring a strategic approach to building partnerships that align with the needs of indie hackers.
