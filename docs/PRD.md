# Product Requirements Document: Idea Fathom
## Overview
Idea Fathom is a community-driven platform designed to facilitate the sharing, discussion, and refinement of ideas. The platform aims to provide a structured feedback loop, enabling users to publish, comment, and improve upon ideas in a collaborative environment.

## Problem Statement
The lack of a dedicated platform for sharing and refining ideas can lead to:
- Difficulty in gathering constructive feedback
- Limited visibility and engagement with published ideas
- Inefficient tracking and organization of feedback and suggestions

## Target Users
- Innovators and entrepreneurs seeking feedback on their ideas
- Community members interested in contributing to the development of new ideas
- Organizations looking to foster a culture of innovation and collaboration

## Goals
- Provide a user-friendly platform for publishing and discussing ideas
- Foster a community that encourages constructive feedback and collaboration
- Enable users to track and refine their ideas based on feedback and suggestions

## Key Features
### Prioritized Features
1. **Idea Publishing**: Users can publish ideas to a public feed with optional anonymity
2. **Commenting and Feedback**: Users can comment, upvote, or suggest improvements on published ideas
3. **Idea Record Management**: Store and link feedback to the original idea record
4. **User Profiles**: Users can create profiles to showcase their published ideas and engagement history
5. **Notification System**: Users receive notifications for comments, suggestions, and upvotes on their ideas

### Non-Prioritized Features (Future Development)
- Integration with popular social media platforms for increased visibility
- Gamification elements to encourage user engagement
- Advanced analytics for idea trends and user behavior

## Success Metrics
- Number of published ideas and user engagement (comments, upvotes, suggestions)
- User retention and return rate
- Quality and constructiveness of feedback and suggestions
- User satisfaction surveys and feedback

## Scope
- Development of the Idea Fathom platform with the prioritized features
- Design and implementation of the user interface and user experience
- Testing and quality assurance of the platform

## Out-of-Scope
- Integration with third-party services (e.g., social media, email marketing)
- Development of a mobile application
- Advanced analytics and data science features

## Acceptance Criteria
- The platform allows users to publish ideas and engage with others' ideas
- The platform stores and links feedback to the original idea record
- The platform provides a user-friendly interface for navigating and interacting with ideas
- The platform meets the defined success metrics and user satisfaction targets
